CALE STREET PARTNERS

Cale Street Partners LLP is a European-focused real estate capital provider that advises Cale Street Investments LP in providing creative, “one-stop” solutions to best-in-class developers, operators, and institutions. The partnerships were founded in 2014 and seeded with approximately $1.5 billion from a sovereign wealth investor with a long term patient approach to investing. Cale Street has a flexible mandate which includes both senior and mezzanine debt finance as well as joint venture equity investment.
